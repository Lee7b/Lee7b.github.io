About me

Hi, I'm Sam Burkett. I was born and currently live in the state of Arkansas. With that said, I'm a huge Razorback fan of all sports and when I'm not working I'm most likely spending time with family or friends. I also love all things tech. I've even been building custom PCs since I was 10 years old! I owe a huge thanks to my grandfather for teaching me about PCs and tech growing up. Without him, I wouldn't be where I am today.

Professionally, I currently work as a Java developer. Primarily developing REST APIs, but occasionally do front-end development as well. I love what I do and make it a goal to improve and better myself every day, both at work and my personal life. I have a computer science degree from UCA, and a passion for programming, problem solving, and learning! The languages/technologies that I use day to day in my job right now are Java, Javascript, SpringBoot, MongoDB, and Oracle.

If you're interested in my resume please get in contact with me either by email or through LinkedIn.
